The Pittsburgh Peguins' Over Time

The Pittsburgh Penguins are a storied and historic franchise within the National Hockey League. They were part of the NHL's first expansion in 1967. This expansion grew the league from the orginial 6 teams to 12 teams. The Penguins' first arena was called many things throughout its history but the name "The Igloo" stuck. What was once a concert venue would now play host to the National Hockey League. With an arena called "The Igloo", the name "Penguins" seemed too perfect.
